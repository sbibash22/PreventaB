About PreventaB

PreventaB is an explainable AI monitoring and alert platform that collects CPU, RAM and Disk telemetry and Windows event logs to predict computer system failures before they happen. It shows risk as LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH and explains why the risk increased using simple English insights, helping administrators take preventive action and reduce downtime.

Explainable AI (XAI) Telemetry + Event Logs Risk Alerts + Email Django + PostgreSQL

Aim

The aim of PreventaB is to research, design, and develop an explainable AI system that analyses computer performance metrics and event logs to predict failures early, generate human-readable explanations, and send early alerts. This helps administrators take preventive steps to minimize downtime and enhance reliability.

What PreventaB does

  • Records CPU, RAM and Disk usage (telemetry)
  • Reads Windows event logs (critical, error, warning, info)
  • Stores collected data safely in PostgreSQL
  • Shows dashboards with charts, logs and risk score
  • Sends alerts via in-app notifications and email
  • Explains risk reasons using XAI methods (SHAP / LIME)

How it works (simple flow)

1) Collect: Agent/collector reads telemetry + logs from Windows device
2) Store: Data is saved into PostgreSQL database (structured records)
3) Predict: Model generates risk score & risk label (LOW/MEDIUM/HIGH)
4) Explain: SHAP/LIME highlights key metrics/log patterns causing risk
5) Alert: System notifies admin/users via dashboard + email (if enabled)

Key objectives

  • Build data pipeline for metrics + error logs → database
  • Clean, parse and feature-engineer dataset for training
  • Train baseline models and advanced models for prediction
  • Use SHAP/LIME for explainable insights in simple English
  • Visualize trends & risk score with dashboard charts
  • Send alerts for high risk with cooldown control
  • Evaluate models using precision, recall, F1, ROC-AUC
  • Document results for future tuning and evaluation

Tech stack

  • Django (web app + authentication)
  • PostgreSQL (data storage)
  • Python / Jupyter (ML training & evaluation)
  • Chart.js (dashboard visualization)
  • SHAP / LIME (explainable AI)

Why PreventaB

PreventaB focuses on reducing failures and downtime by turning raw telemetry and log patterns into actionable risk insights. By pairing prediction with explainability, it helps users understand what happened, why it happened, and what to do next (prevention actions).

Admin benefits

Central control, device monitoring, risk thresholds, alerts, and audit logs.

User benefits

Personal device monitoring, clear charts, risk insights, and notifications.
